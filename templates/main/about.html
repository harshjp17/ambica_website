Who We Are

Shree Ambica Engineering

Founded in 1997 in Dhansura, Gujarat, Shree Ambica Engineering was built on a single-minded focus: to manufacture high-quality wire mesh screens and perforated plates that keep India's mining and quarry plants running at full capacity.

Over more than 27 years, we have supplied thousands of screening panels to stone crushers, mining plants, coal handling units, and aggregate processors across every region of India — from Gujarat and Rajasthan to Jharkhand, Chhattisgarh, and Maharashtra.

Our manufacturing unit is equipped with precision wire weaving machines, heavy-duty punching presses, and in-house carbon coating lines — enabling complete vertical integration with strict quality control at every stage.

Foundation
